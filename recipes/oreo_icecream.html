Three ingredient Oreo ice cream recipe

This easy ice cream dessert recipe requires only 3 ingredients and is delicious!

ingredients

    2 Cups of heavy cream
    1 can sweetened condensed milk
    10-12 Oreo cookies (crushed)

Preparation

1. Whip the cream In a large bowl whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form (about 3-5 minutes) .

2. Mix with sweetened condensed milkIn another bowl, pour the sweetened condensed milk and gently fold in the whipped cream until fully combined.

3. Add OreosCrush the Oreo cookies into small chunks or crumbs. Add them to the mixture and mix them in gently

4. FreezePour the mixture into a loaf pan or an airtight container. Cover and freeze for at least 4-6 hours, or until fully set (overnight works best).

7. Enjoy!
